### 3. Relationship State

Observation rules:

```text
long  = active downtrend channel + touched bullish OB + touched bullish FVG
short = active uptrend channel   + touched bearish OB + touched bearish FVG
```

Initial diagnostic scores:

```text
2 = trend channel + one compatible touched zone
3 = trend channel + overlapping OB + FVG
```

Correction after chart review:

```text
state=1 only when score=3
```

Two-evidence combinations were too noisy and are no longer emitted as
relationship states. The previous rolling three-detector count remains
available as a diagnostic value but no longer drives live `state=1`.
